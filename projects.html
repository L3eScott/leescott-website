What Happens When Your Business Has the Right Systems

The businesses below aren't special. They didn't have unlimited budgets or large teams. They had the same problems most businesses face — and we built systems to solve them.

HVAC & Home Services Company — Houston, TX

AI Lead Automation

The Problem

A home services company was losing leads after hours and on weekends. Their website had no way to capture visitors who weren't ready to call, and their manual follow-up process meant interested prospects heard back 12–24 hours later — often after they'd already booked a competitor.

What We Built

A smart website with a 24/7 AI chat widget that captures lead information at any hour, an automated follow-up email sequence triggered the moment someone submits, and instant SMS notifications to the owner for every new lead. All integrated into a simple CRM pipeline.

The Shift

The business stopped losing leads to faster competitors. The owner no longer manually monitors the inbox for new inquiries. The system follows up — immediately, consistently, and professionally — whether it's 2pm or 2am.

312% Increase in leads captured
< 2 min Average lead response time
40% Reduction in missed inquiries
24/7 Lead capture, no manual effort

Executive Coaching Practice — Austin, TX

Booking & Follow-Up Automation

The Problem

An executive coach was spending 6+ hours every week on scheduling, email follow-up, and client onboarding — none of which required their expertise. Administrative overhead was eating into billable time and creating a poor first impression for new clients.

What We Built

An online booking system embedded directly into the website, automated welcome and onboarding email sequences for new clients, follow-up reminders for prospects who hadn't booked, and a streamlined intake process that gathered client info before the first session.

The Shift

The coach reclaimed nearly a full workday each week. New clients receive a consistent, professional onboarding experience from the moment they book. The calendar fills itself. The coach focuses on coaching.

8 hrs Saved per week
100% Automated client onboarding
28% Increase in bookings
0 Scheduling emails sent manually

Commercial Real Estate Group — Dallas-Fort Worth, TX

Reporting & Business Intelligence

The Problem

A commercial real estate investment group had data spread across multiple spreadsheets and systems with no unified view of portfolio performance. Monthly reporting took 20+ hours to compile, decisions were made on data that was weeks old, and leadership lacked confidence in what they were seeing.

What We Built

A Power BI reporting dashboard connected to their existing data sources, an automated data pipeline that refreshes daily without manual input, and a clear set of KPIs and visualizations that give leadership a real-time view of portfolio health, occupancy, and revenue.

The Shift

Leadership stopped waiting for monthly reports to make decisions. The data is always current, always accessible, and presented in a format that's clear to executives without a data background. Reporting time dropped from 20 hours to near zero.

22 hrs Saved per month on reporting
Real-time Portfolio visibility
Daily Automated data refresh
15% Improvement in decision speed
