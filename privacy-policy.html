Last updated: April 22, 2026

Digital Ops Systems ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website at digitalopsystems.com. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

We collect information you provide directly, such as your name, email address, phone number, and business details when you fill out our contact form or request a consultation.

We also collect standard analytics data automatically — pages visited, time on site, device type, and general location — through Google Analytics.

How We Use Your Information
