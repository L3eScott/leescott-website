How It Works

Four steps from here to funded.

Our process is designed to be clear, fast, and completely on your terms. No pressure — just a straightforward path to the funds you need.

No Hard Credit Pull Pre-qualification uses only a soft inquiry — zero impact to your credit score.
Decisions in 24 Hours Most personal loan applications receive a decision the same business day.
No Obligation to Accept We present your options and let you decide. No pressure, no time limits.
1
Step One

Schedule a Free Consultation

Connect with a licensed lending advisor by phone, video, or in-person at our Atlanta office. There's no cost, no obligation, and no credit check at this stage. We learn about your goals and your situation — that's it.

Takes 20–30 minutes
2
Step Two

Review Your Personalized Options

We run a soft credit check and match your profile against our network of over 40 lending partners. You'll see real rates, real terms, and clear side-by-side comparisons — presented by a human, not a portal. You ask questions, we answer them.

Results within 2–4 hours
3
Step Three

Submit Your Application

Once you've chosen an offer, our team handles document gathering, application completion, and lender submission. We stay on top of the process and keep you updated at every stage — no chasing required on your end.

Typically 1–3 business days
4
Step Four

Receive Your Funds

Upon approval and signing, funds are deposited directly to your account — often within one to two business days. We follow up after funding to make sure everything went smoothly and answer any questions about your first payment.

1–2 business days after approval
About Clearpath

Built by people who
know what it's like to need a loan.

Clearpath Lending Group was founded in 2011 by a team of former bank loan officers who were tired of watching good people get declined for technical reasons — or worse, get steered into products that weren't right for them.

We built Clearpath to be the advisor we wished existed: licensed, independent, and working for the borrower rather than the lender. We're paid a flat fee by our lending partners — never a commission based on loan size — which means our recommendations are based on what's right for your financial situation, period.

Headquartered in Atlanta with advisors serving clients across 12 states, we've helped over 15,000 families and businesses access more than $850 million in funding since our founding.

Why Clearpath

What makes us different isn't a slogan.

It's the way we work. Here's what clients consistently tell us sets Clearpath apart from the bank, the app, and the competition.

A Dedicated Human Advisor

You'll work with one advisor from first call to funded — not a queue, not a chatbot. Your advisor learns your situation and advocates for your best outcome every step of the way.

Flat-Fee, Unbiased Recommendations

We earn a flat referral fee from lenders — not a percentage of your loan. That means we have no incentive to push you toward a larger or more expensive product than you need.

Access to 40+ Lending Partners

We work with banks, credit unions, community development lenders, and specialty finance companies. That means more programs, lower minimums, and real options for borrowers banks often decline.

No Prepayment Penalties

Every loan we facilitate comes with confirmed no-prepayment-penalty terms. Pay your loan off early, make extra payments, or pay in full — you keep the savings. Always.

Fast, Transparent Timelines

We quote realistic timelines and meet them. From soft-pull pre-qualification to final approval, you always know where you stand and what comes next — in plain language.

Local Presence, Statewide Reach

Our Atlanta headquarters serves as a real office with real people. We serve clients across 12 states by phone and video, but we've never outsourced customer service offshore. Our team is here.
