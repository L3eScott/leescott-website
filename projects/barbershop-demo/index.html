Our Story

A Cut Above the
Ordinary Barbershop

The Meridian was born from a simple idea: men deserve a barbershop experience that takes them seriously. Not a franchise. Not a 15-minute in-and-out. A real shop with skilled barbers, quality products, and a chair you actually want to sit in.

Founded by Marcus Webb in Houston's Midtown neighborhood, we've spent over a decade building relationships — with clients who return not just for the cut, but for the conversation and the consistency.

✦
Precision as a Practice Every cut is taken seriously. We don't rush, and it shows.
🪒
Old-School Craft, Modern Taste Classic techniques. Current styles. No excuses for mediocre work.
🤝
The Same Chair, Every Time We know your hair — and your name — because repeat clients are our foundation.

The Barbers

Meet the Team

Three barbers. One standard. Every chair at The Meridian is held to the same level of skill and care.

👨🏾
Marcus Webb
Owner & Master Barber
15 years behind the chair. Marcus trained in New York before opening The Meridian. His specialty is fades, but ask anyone — his scissor work is what keeps clients coming back.
👨🏻
Dante Cruz
Senior Barber
Dante brings an artistic eye and a steady hand. Known for clean line work and a mastery of beard sculpting that makes clients look ten years younger.
👱🏽
Jordan Ellis
Barber & Color Specialist
Jordan handles color, texture, and gray blending with a light touch that looks natural every time. His cuts are clean and his gray blends are borderline undetectable.
