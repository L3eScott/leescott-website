The Harlow Difference

A venue built for
extraordinary moments.

Every detail at The Harlow has been considered — from the grand entrance to the hidden garden terrace — to ensure your event feels effortless and unforgettable.

Grand Ballroom

Our 8,500 sq ft ballroom features 22-foot coffered ceilings, warm hardwood floors, and a dramatic chandelier installation that transforms any event into something cinematic.

Garden Terrace

Step outside to our wisteria-draped Garden Terrace — a 3,500 sq ft outdoor sanctuary surrounded by mature oaks and manicured hedgerows. Perfect for cocktail hours and ceremonies.

Dedicated Suites

A private bridal suite and separate groom's lounge — each with ensuite baths, vanity stations, and a private outdoor balcony — give your wedding party a serene retreat on the day.

Full Production Ready

Integrated 4K AV systems, architectural uplighting with 16 million color options, built-in stage and rigging points, and a professional sound suite — all in-house, no rentals needed.

Chef's Kitchen

Our commercial-grade kitchen is outfitted for professional catering operations at any scale — accommodating your preferred caterer or choosing from our curated list of preferred culinary partners.

Secured & Staffed

On-site event coordinators, valet parking for up to 400 vehicles, and dedicated venue security ensure your guests are welcomed and your event runs without interruption from arrival to farewell.

Flexible Scheduling

Exclusive venue access from setup to teardown, with flexible booking windows from intimate four-hour celebrations to multi-day corporate retreats. We work around your vision, not a rigid template.

Preferred Vendor Access

From florists and photographers to entertainment and transportation, our curated network of over 40 vetted partners means you have access to Franklin's finest professionals at every budget level.
