Our Story

More Than a
Salon Visit.

Atelier Silk was founded by Simone Carter in 2012 with a single belief: every woman deserves a stylist who truly listens. Not someone who talks you into something. Someone who understands your hair — its history, its texture, its potential.

Simone trained in New York and Atlanta before bringing her craft back to Houston's Midtown neighborhood. Today, Atelier Silk is a team of four licensed stylists, all handpicked for their technical skill and their ability to make clients feel completely at ease.

Hair Health First Every service starts with the health of your hair, not what looks fastest on Instagram.
Appointments That Respect Your Time We are known for running on schedule. Because your time is as valuable as ours.
A Space Where You Exhale From the moment you walk in, this is your hour. No rush, no noise, just good work done well.
