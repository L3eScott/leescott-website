About Sloane

Fashion. Travel.
Real Life.

I'm Sloane — a Los Angeles-based content creator, style curator, and brand storyteller with an obsession for experiences that actually matter. I built my audience the old-fashioned way: by showing up every single day with content that's equal parts beautiful and honest.

From boutique hotels in Positano to morning routines in my Silver Lake apartment, my content lives at the intersection of elevated aesthetics and genuine living. My community trusts my recommendations because they know I'd never put my name on something I don't believe in.

When I'm not filming, you'll find me at a farmers market, deep in a design exhibition, or hunting for the best natural wine in whatever city I happen to be in that week.
