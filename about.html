About Lee Scott

Business Intelligence Specialist & AI Automation Architect serving Texas businesses ready to grow.

Lee Scott

Data-Driven Business Automation Architect

For over a decade, I've been helping businesses make better decisions through data — and now I help them act on those decisions automatically.

My background is in Business Intelligence: Power BI dashboards, SQL databases, reporting systems, and the data infrastructure that gives leaders a clear view of what's actually happening in their business. Over time, I saw a consistent pattern. Business owners had data. They just didn't have systems to act on it — fast enough, consistently enough, or without burning hours of manual work every week.

That's what led me to AI automation. When you combine clear data with smart automation, something changes for a business. Leads get followed up automatically. Appointments get booked without back-and-forth. Reports run themselves. The business starts operating the way it should — like a system, not a scramble.

Today I build the Smart Website + AI Workers System: a professional digital presence with automation built directly into it, so your business captures leads, nurtures prospects, and operates efficiently — with or without you in the room.

I work with entrepreneurs, small business owners, and corporate executives across Houston, Austin, San Antonio, and Dallas-Fort Worth — and remotely with clients nationwide.

Mission

Why I Do This Work

I believe every business — not just the large ones — deserves the same digital infrastructure that enterprise companies take for granted. The technology exists. The problem is access and implementation.

Small and mid-size business owners are some of the hardest-working people in any economy. They carry the operations, the sales, the client work — often all at once. My job is to build the systems that absorb that burden and let them focus on what they do best.

A website that works like an employee. A business that follows up like a team. A system that reports like an analyst. That's the standard every client deserves.
