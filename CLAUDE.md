# CLAUDE.md

## Project
This project is the website for Lee Scott.

## Business Positioning
Lee Scott is a Data-Driven Business Automation Architect Leveraging AI Systems.

## Core Concept
A website is the digital brick-and-mortar.
AI automations are the workers inside the digital building.

## Core Message
We build the digital building and the workers inside it.

## Primary Product
Smart Website + AI Workers

## Product Description
A smart website system that helps businesses capture leads, follow up automatically, generate content, improve visibility, and operate more intelligently online.

## Revenue Goal
$100,000 within 12 months.

## Audience
Entrepreneurs, small businesses, corporate leaders, executives, and organizations seeking automation and intelligent digital systems.

## Regional Focus
Houston, Austin, San Antonio, Dallas-Fort Worth.

## Design Rules
- Keep the site simple HTML/CSS/JS for now.
- Use one consistent header, nav, footer, spacing, and visual style across all pages.
- Use navy (#0D1B2A), gold (#C9A84C), and white.
- Make the site modern, clear, business-focused, and reassuring.
- Do not make it feel overly technical.

## Messaging Rules
- Focus on outcomes: more customers, less missed opportunity, faster follow-up, saving time.
- Businesses do not care about AI by itself.
- The website must explain the offer fast.
- The website should communicate that we help a business website act like a working employee, not a brochure.
